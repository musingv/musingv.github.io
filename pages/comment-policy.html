Comment Policy

Welcome to Pondering Troth. The comment philosophy is pretty simple: Be respectful.

Given that I write about truth, faith, and other sensitive topics, I encourage serious and thoughtful dialog - understanding and listening to others, expressing one’s own notions, and exploring ideas’ strengths, weaknesses, and limitations.

Pro Tip

Check out logical fallacies for ideas on how to steelman your writing.

The above is not an excuse to attack, troll, or spam people. Please help create the kind of community that treats others the way you’d want to be treated.

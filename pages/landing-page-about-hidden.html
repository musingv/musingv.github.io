Quick Introduction

Welcome to Pondering Truth. I write about what interests me – mostly about what faithfully following Jesus means and how it intersects with American evangelical culture, mixing in other fields such as software design and user experience.

Along the way, I touch on plenty of hot topics such as politics, race, gender, and mental health. So if you stick around very long, you’ll probably be offended, and I’ll certainly be completely wrong about something.
